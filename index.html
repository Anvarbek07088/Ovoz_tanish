Transkript

Salom, men O'zbek tili uchun avtomatik nutqni tanib olish tizimi ustida ishlayapman. Bu tajriba uchun audio yozuv.

5.2 soniya 15 so'z 16000Hz

Matematik model

MFCC chiqarish
MFCC = DCT(log(|FFT(x(t))|²))

Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients - audio signalning spektral xususiyatlari

CTC loss funksiyasi
LCTC = -log P(y|x) = -log ∑π∈B⁻¹(y) P(π|x)
WER hisoblash
WER = (S + D + I) / N × 100%

S - almashtirish, D - o'chirish, I - qo'shish, N - haqiqiy so'zlar soni
